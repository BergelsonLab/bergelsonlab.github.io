Cross-continental language input and production study featured in 'Harvard Gazette'

Cross-continental language input and production study featured in 'Science'

  • 12/20/23
A recently-published collaboration between our lab and scientists around the world finds that more talkative parents are associated with more talkative children; no effect of socioeconomic status on children's talk. The study, published in PNAS, analyzed day-long recordings from 1,001 children from 6 continents!
