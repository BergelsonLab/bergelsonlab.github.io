Bergelson Lab

Harvard University's Laboratory for Developmental Studies

The Bergelson Lab (aka BLAB ) is a part of the Laboratory for Developmental Studies group within the Department of Psychology at Harvard University. We are located on the 11th floor at William James Hall.

We study infant word learning, in particular how infantsâ€™ early linguistic and environmental input plays a role in their learning. We focus on understanding how babies learn words from the visual, social, and linguistic world around them.

The BLAB moved to Harvard from Duke University in 2023.
