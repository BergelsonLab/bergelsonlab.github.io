How to get to the Bergelson Lab

We are located on Duke's West Campus near the Sarah P. Duke Gardens. If using GPS, you can navigate to Duke University Department of Psychology and Neuroscience. A researcher will meet you at the 25mph sign near the entrance to the Allen Lower Parking Lot on Flowers Drive. If you are having trouble finding us, feel free to call the lab at (919)684-9429.
